Baked Meatballs

From: http://www.food.com/recipe/baked-meatballs-182907

Ingredients:

* 1 1/4 lbs ground sirloin
* 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
* 1 egg, beaten
* 1/2 cup seasoned bread crumbs
* 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
* 2 garlic cloves
* salt and pepper

Directions:

1. Preheat oven to 425F
2. Mix ingredients together in large bowl with hands, forming 1 1/2 inch balls
3. Place on greased cookie sheet and bake 10-15 minutes
